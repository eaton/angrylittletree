It's pretty wild how good things really can be when you're with someone you love and they're healthy and whole and they laugh at the crazy things you do. It's been ages and ages since a proper update, though, and to that end I present two groups of photos: the wedding of <a href="http://js0n.livejournal.com">js0n</a> and <a href="http://breezygirl.livejournal.com">breezygirl</a>, and the glorious, gorgeous snow that coated the Chicagoland area on Thanksgiving eve.
