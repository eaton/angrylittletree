Met up with Catherine again after she got off work yesterday, and meandered into Hinckley. It's a smallish town of 2000, technically a suburb of Chicago but in reality far outside the city's gravity. It's a treasure trove of texture and rhythm and greenery and all sorts of goodness. She shows me the coolest things.
