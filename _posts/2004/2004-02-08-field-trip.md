So this morning, my alarm went off at an ungodly 7:00 AM. Why? Photography class field trip! We all piled into a van and drove up to the Chicago Botanical Gardens. Though they're not in their full summer glory, the facility has numerous greenhouses for year-round climate simulation. Cacti? Got 'em. Banana trees? Got 'em. Flowers in February? Got 'em. And get 'em we did.
